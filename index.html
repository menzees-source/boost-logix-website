Getting Started

Three Steps to Full Insight

From EvoScan export to full tune analysis in under a minute.

01
Log Your Run

Use EvoScan to log your WOT pull, idle session, or cruise run. Boost LogiX supports all three log types and auto-detects which one you've loaded.

02
Drop the CSV

Drag and drop your EvoScan CSV export directly into Boost LogiX. The app parses, validates, and charts everything instantly — no setup required.

03
Read the Data

Review AFR, knock, boost, timing, fuel trims, and injector duty — colour-coded alerts flag anything that needs attention. Export a full PDF report.
